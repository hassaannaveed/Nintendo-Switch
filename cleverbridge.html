Cleverbridge

Solutions for Cleverbridge for their diverse customer base.

Zoom Function

Clever Bridge can use a Zoom function like the one present in Nintendo Switch to assist the customers better and target a wide range of audience of those who are short-sighted.

Colorblind Mode

Clever Bridge can use a Colorblind mode like the one present in Nintendo Switch to assist the customers better and also help the disabled employees to work efficiently in the company.

Control Sensitivity Adjustments

Clever Bridge can allow users to adjust control sensitivity, helping those with motor impairments navigate the platform more comfortably and efficiently.

Subtitles and Speech-to-Text

By adding subtitles and speech-to-text features, Clever Bridge can assist customers with hearing impairments and improve communication during support interactions.
